SPECIAL EVENT SHUTTLE – COVID-19 VACCINATIONS

ROBERT CABRAL AGRICULTURAL CENTER

TUESDAY TO FRIDAY

(WEEKDAY ONLY)

In supporting the community, RTD will offer free rides on fixed route service for everyone who needs to get a COVID-19 vaccine at the Robert Cabral Agricultural Center, 2101 E. Earhart Avenue.

A shuttle service will be available from the Union Transfer Station (UTS) to the Agricultural Center.

When boarding, please show proof of appointment to the operator.
